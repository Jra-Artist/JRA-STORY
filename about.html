Once upon a time there was a monkey tribe that lived in peace and harmony.
The Monkey King was a protector of all living beings, using his power and his magic stick.

One day, the king suffered from a disease that had no cure.
The Monkey King was slowly weakening—he could no longer fight.

The Monkey King’s wife had to take care of her husband due to the disease.
The world of all living beings lost their powerful protector.
The Monkey King died... (Who will protect us now?)
